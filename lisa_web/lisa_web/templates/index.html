LISA online

TF ranking inference and enhancer identification from large scale epigenome data

Overview

Large scale In-Silico Analysis of Chromatin profile for inference of Gene Regulation

The motivation of LISA is to use public along with in-house chromatin profile data from a comprehensive database of human and mouse DNase-seq, and H3K27ac ChIP-seq profiles, to determine the transcription factors and chromatin regulators that are directly responsible for the perturbation of a differentially expressed gene set. To run LISA, the only thing you need is the differential gene list from whatever biological process you are interested in. Then you will be able to leverage the power of the most comprehensive DNase and ChIP-seq database (CistromeDB) to discover the underlying regulatory mechanisms, reveal the key transcription factors and chromatin regulators and also their binding sites!
